The Pocket Spinner
THE POCKET SPINNER is very beneficial in learning proper jump technique. THE POCKET SPINNER allows you to simulate your air position, as you stand on relev√© rather than a flat foot. This is important when jumping, because you should be landing on a pointed foot as opposed to a flat foot. THE POCKET SPINNER also allows you to train twizzles by placing the spinner under the center of your foot. Unlike the majority of off-ice spinners that utilize a bottom rocker to achieve rotation, THE POCKET SPINNER utilizes two machined stainless-steel plates with premium ball bearings between the plates to achieve minimal friction and faster rotation.

Our team
Owner
"My name is Cheri Neset, owner and operator of Dakota Spinners.
I was born and raised in Minot, ND. I started skating when I was 6 years old. I competed in singles, solo dance, and synchronized skating. I competed as a member of Magic Blades, a Junior level synchronized skating team. In 1997, we earned a silver medal at the US Synchronized Skating Championships. The following season, we were assigned to compete at Spring Cup in Milan, Italy, where we placed third. We went on to take home the gold medal at the 1998 US Synchronized Skating Championships. I am a USFS gold medalist in Moves in the Field and Ice Dance and have my Junior FS test.
I started coaching while attending college. I am a PSA rated coach, holding the following ratings: Certified Moves in the Field, Certified Free Skate, and Registered Synchronized Skating.
In 2017, we relocated to Colorado Springs to help our daughter, Leah, pursue her passion in ice dance. She and her partner, Artem Markelov, are currently competing at the Junior level."
- Cheri Neset
